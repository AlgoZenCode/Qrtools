About QRTools

Your fast, secure and privacy-friendly online utility hub. Built with ❤️ by AlgozenCode.

Updated: 7 December 2025

Our Mission

At QRTools by AlgozenCode, our mission is simple: to make powerful digital tools accessible, fast, and extremely easy to use for everyone. In a world where online utilities have become a daily necessity, we believe users deserve tools that are reliable, transparent, and respectful of their privacy.

This website has been created to provide a collection of lightweight, browser-based utilities that help you complete essential tasks such as generating QR codes, hashing text, creating secure passwords, shortening URLs, and more — all without complicated software or account registration.

Privacy First. Always.

One of the core principles behind QRTools is data privacy. We understand that the content you enter into any online tool is often sensitive or personal. That is why our platform is designed from the ground up to handle your data responsibly.

Most of our utilities operate directly inside your browser using client-side scripts. This means the information you enter never even reaches our server in the first place. Whenever backend operations are required for the tool to function, we ensure that your data is never stored permanently.

  • We do not save or log your data in our backend.

    Your information stays private and secure.

  • Automatic data deletion.

    If temporary processing is required, the data is automatically deleted within 24 hours.

  • No data sharing or selling.

    We do not sell, share, or analyze user information.

  • No tracking, no unnecessary cookies.

    No sign-up required, no tracking cookies, just pure utility.

Our goal is to give you peace of mind every time you use our platform. You remain in full control of your information.
